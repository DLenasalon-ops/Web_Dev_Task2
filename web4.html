Web 4.0

Web 4.0 Illustration

Definition

Web 4.0 is the next evolution of the World Wide Web, often referred to as the "Symbiotic Web" or "Web of Things." It envisions a future where the web becomes more intelligent, interconnected, and seamlessly integrated into our daily lives.

Web 4.0 is expected to leverage advanced technologies such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, and the Internet of Things (IoT) to create a more personalized and immersive online experience. It aims to enable smarter interactions between users and digital content, as well as facilitate the integration of physical and digital environments.
